Arroz con leche(Rice pudding)

Cuban-style rice pudding is an easy, refreshing, and creamy dessert that appeals to all Cubans.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup rice
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Vanilla essence
  • Lemon zest

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and water in a pot over medium heat for 20 minutes. (If, after the initial 20 minutes, the rice has not yet softened properly, add another half-cup of water.)
  2. Stir frequently to prevent the mixture from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
  3. Once the rice has softened to a purée-like consistency, add the sugar, milk, and lemon zest.
  4. Cook the rice, milk, and sugar mixture over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring again.
  5. When the mixture has set and thickened, remove from the heat and stir in a teaspoon of vanilla essence.
  6. You can garnish with a few cinnamon sticks.
