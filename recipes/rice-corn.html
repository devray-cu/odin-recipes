Arroz con maiz(Rice with corn)

Rice with corn is a very popular side dish in Cuba. In fact, corn is added to many dishes because it is a vegetable with an exquisite flavor and numerous beneficial properties.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb rice
  • 1 ½ cups corn kernels
  • 1 small onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • ½ chili pepper
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • ½ cup tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Pour two spoonfuls of oil into a pot; add the water, spices, tomato, and chicken bouillon cubes, and cook over high heat until boiling.
  2. Add the rice and stir well with a fork.
  3. Once it starts to boil, lower the heat and cook over medium heat, tightly covered, until the rice is tender.
  4. Then, add the pre-cooked corn, cover, and cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Finally, add a clove of finely crushed garlic and stir.
